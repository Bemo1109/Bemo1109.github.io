About Me

For the longest, I've questioned what exactly my passion is. Going down the list of my interests I was unsure I'd be able to settle on one. That was until I saw an ad on social media for coding. I had never done coding and unsure if I'd even like but I figured I'd at least give it a shot. Fast forward a year later, and here I am confidently creating a website from scratch. Moral of the story, don't be afraid to step outside of your comfort zone you might just find your passion.

If you like what I've done the blog or want some work done for yourself you can click the contact link in the navigation bar. I'm always looking for ways to to apply what I've learned. Thanks a bunch for checking out my little project.
